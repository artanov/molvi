How it works

Three moves, and you're done

No windows or buttons while you work. One key, your voice, and text.

Hold the key

Left Ctrl by default. You can assign any combination in settings.

Speak

A pill-shaped overlay shows a recording is in progress. Speak naturally, at your own pace.

Release

Molvi recognizes your speech and types it straight into the active window, wherever your cursor is.

Features

Thought through down to the details

Everything you need for everyday dictation — and nothing you don't.

Privacy

Audio never leaves your computer. Works even without internet.

Any hotkey

The dictation key is configurable — from a single Ctrl to your own combination.

Microphone and language

Choose your recording device and language: Russian, English, or auto-detect.

GPU and CPU

With an NVIDIA GPU — maximum quality; without one — a fast model on the CPU.

Overlay and sounds

See and hear when recording is happening and when text is being recognized.

Launch at startup

One checkbox, and Molvi is always ready in the tray once your PC boots.

Vibe coding

Start dictating in a couple of minutes

Windows 10/11 x64 (maximum quality with an NVIDIA GPU with 6+ GB) or a Mac with any M-series chip: M1, M2, M3, M4 and newer — recognition runs through Metal, even a base M1 with 8 GB is enough. Intel Macs are not supported. First launch detects your hardware and downloads the model automatically.
